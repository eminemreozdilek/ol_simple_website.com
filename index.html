About OpenLattice


OpenLattice is a software developed for academic and research purposes to streamline the design of Triply Periodic Minimal Surface (TPMS) lattice structures. With its user-friendly graphical user interface, OpenLattice offers three key features: uniform TPMS generation, anisotropic homogenization, and homogenization-based topology optimization (HMTO). The software effectively generates both uniform and functionally graded lattice designs with high accuracy in geometric dimensions. Additionally, OpenLattice facilitates the export of designed structures in .stl and common finite element (FE) formats for additive manufacturing and subsequent finite element analysis, providing a valuable resource for the complexities of designing lattice structures and enhancing their adoption in research fields.

The team is open to sharing the source codes with other researchers or research groups for collaboration purposes. This openness fosters collaboration, allowing for continuous improvement and adaptation of the software to meet the evolving needs of the academic and research communities.
